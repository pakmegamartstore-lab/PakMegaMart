## ANIMATION EFFECTS

### Form Transitions
```
Step 1 → Step 2:  Fade out + Slide left
Step 2 → Step 3:  Fade out + Slide left
Step 3 ← Step 2:  Fade in + Slide right
Step 2 ← Step 1:  Fade in + Slide right
```

### Button Hover Effects
```
Normal:   Gray background
Hover:    Gradient color + Lift up
Active:   Darker gradient + Pressed
```

### Cart Notification
```
Appears:  Slide in from right
Duration: 3 seconds
Exits:    Fade out + Slide right
```

### Success Modal
```
Appears:  Scale up + Fade in
Background: Black overlay at 50% opacity
Duration:   Persistent until dismissed
```
