Our Story

A journey through four decades of passion, tradition, and excellence in leather craftsmanship.

Rooted in Heritage

PakMegaMart was born from a deep commitment to quality and integrity—values deeply rooted in Pakistani tradition. Inspired by the rich leatherworking heritage of Karachi and Lahore, our founder Hassan Malik brought together master craftsmen to create wallets that reflect our national pride in exceptional craftsmanship. Each piece is created with the same dedication our forefathers applied to their work.

In 2018, Hassan established our atelier with a vision to honor the time-tested techniques of traditional Pakistani leatherwork while bringing global quality standards. We believe in creating products that can be passed down through families—true heirlooms that carry the dignity and respect our culture holds dear.

"ہر ٹانکا ایک کہانی ہے، ہر چمڑا اپنی شان رکھتا ہے۔ ہمارا کام یہ شان کو احترام سے بحال رکھنا ہے۔" (Every stitch is a story; every leather has its own dignity. Our duty is to honor that with respect.)

- Hassan Malik, Founder

Our Journey

2018 - ہمارا آغاز (The Beginning)

Hassan Malik establishes PakMegaMart in Karachi, uniting master craftsmen to bring world-class leather goods to Pakistan and the world, honoring our nation's artisanal heritage.

2019 - قومی معیار (National Quality Standards)

We source premium leather from certified tanneries across Pakistan, ensuring ethical practices and supporting local industries while maintaining international quality benchmarks.

2020 - نئی نسل (Next Generation Training)

Our apprenticeship program launches, training young Pakistani craftsmen in time-honored techniques, ensuring our heritage continues to thrive for generations to come.

2021 - ماحول دوست (Eco-Conscious Practices)

We commit to sustainable practices, using responsibly sourced materials and minimalist packaging, reflecting our values of respect for nature and future generations.

2023 - جدید رابطہ (Modern Connection)

Launch of our digital platform, bringing traditional Pakistani craftsmanship to customers across Pakistan and beyond, while staying true to our artisanal values.

2026 - حکمت اور عمل (Wisdom & Excellence)

Today, our team of 35+ master craftsmen continues creating exceptional leather wallets that represent the best of Pakistani craftsmanship, earning trust from customers across the nation and abroad.

The Art of Craftsmanship

Every wallet undergoes a meticulous process that combines centuries-old techniques with modern precision.

1

Selection

We carefully select only the finest full-grain hides from our trusted Italian tanneries, ensuring perfect texture and durability.

2

Cutting

Each piece is hand-cut using traditional patterns, with our artisans carefully working around natural markings to showcase the leather's character.

3

Stitching

Using waxed linen thread and traditional saddle stitching, our artisans create seams that are both beautiful and incredibly strong.

4

Finishing

Edges are hand-burnished to a smooth finish, and the entire piece is treated with natural oils to enhance the leather's natural beauty.
