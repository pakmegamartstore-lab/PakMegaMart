# Premium Leather Wallets - Visual Design Philosophy

## Design Philosophy

### Color Palette
**Primary Colors:**
- Rich Cognac (#B8860B) - Warm, sophisticated leather tones
- Deep Burgundy (#722F37) - Luxury wine-inspired accent
- Charcoal (#36454F) - Professional, timeless base
- Warm Cream (#F5F5DC) - Clean background, leather parchment feel

**Supporting Colors:**
- Saddle Brown (#8B4513) - Natural leather variation
- Forest Green (#355E3B) - Masculine accent for exotic leathers
- Soft Gold (#DAA520) - Metallic accents for premium details

### Typography
**Display Font:** Playfair Display (Serif) - Elegant, editorial luxury feel
**Body Font:** Inter (Sans-serif) - Clean, modern readability
**Accent Font:** Crimson Text (Serif) - For quotes and craftsmanship details

### Visual Language
**Heritage Luxury Aesthetic:**
- Rich textures that evoke the tactile quality of fine leather
- Warm, earthy tones that mirror natural leather patina
- Clean geometric layouts with generous white space
- Subtle shadows and depth to create premium feel
- Photography-focused design showcasing craftsmanship details

## Visual Effects & Styling

### Used Libraries & Effects
1. **Anime.js** - Smooth micro-interactions and hover animations
2. **Pixi.js** - Leather texture overlays and premium visual effects
3. **ECharts.js** - Price range sliders and product comparison charts
4. **Splide.js** - Elegant product carousels and image galleries
5. **Typed.js** - Typewriter effect for craftsmanship storytelling
6. **Splitting.js** - Letter-by-letter animations for headings
7. **p5.js** - Interactive leather texture generation

### Header Effects
**Leather Texture Background:**
- Subtle animated leather grain using Pixi.js
- Warm lighting effects that shift like natural patina
- Parallax scrolling with depth layers
- No color masks - pure texture and lighting

### Animation Style
**Micro-interactions:**
- Gentle fade-ins for product reveals
- Smooth scale transforms on hover (1.02x)
- Color transitions that mirror leather aging
- Staggered animations for product grids
- Smooth cart interactions with leather-like elasticity

### Scroll Motion
**Reveal Animations:**
- Elements fade in from 20px below
- Trigger when element enters top 60% of viewport
- 200ms duration with ease-out timing
- Staggered delays for card grids (50ms intervals)

### Hover Effects
**Product Cards:**
- Subtle lift with soft shadow expansion
- Image zoom (1.05x scale) with overlay reveal
- Color-shifting borders in cognac tones
- Smooth transitions maintaining luxury feel

**Interactive Elements:**
- Buttons: Color morphing from charcoal to burgundy
- Links: Underline animation with leather-stitch appearance
- Filters: Gentle glow effects in warm tones
- Cart icon: Bounce animation with leather texture

### Background Design
**Consistent Leather-Inspired Background:**
- Continuous warm cream base throughout all pages
- Subtle leather grain texture overlay
- No section-specific background colors
- Decorative elements using leather tooling patterns
- Geometric shapes in cognac and burgundy as accents

### Product Photography Style
**Hero & Product Images:**
- Dramatic lighting emphasizing leather texture
- Warm, natural lighting (golden hour aesthetic)
- Close-up detail shots showing stitching and grain
- Lifestyle photography in masculine, refined settings
- Consistent warm color grading throughout

### Craftsmanship Storytelling
**Visual Narrative Elements:**
- Process imagery: tanning, stitching, finishing
- Artisan hands at work (close-up, dramatic lighting)
- Tool and material textures
- Before/after patina development
- Heritage workshop environments
